About Me

Electrical and Electronic Engineering Graduate

Electrical Engineering has always been an interesting and appealing subject for me. The fact that the foundation of modern life is based upon electrical engineering has always fascinated me throughout my growing years. Tinkering with different electronic components from different toys and old electrical devices has made me interested more, and wanted to learn how it all works. These have worked as building blocks for me to take up Electrical Engineering as my field of study and completed my bachelor’s degree in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from BRAC University recently. I am an enthusiastic, self-motivated, reliable, responsible and hard working person, and able to work well both in a team environment as well as using own initiative. Currently, I am a PhD student and Graduate Assistant in Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Memphis
